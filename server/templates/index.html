SeeMyFeels

Ever really been into a song? Stuck in your feels and want a way to remember that track? Make it memorable, with a unique piece of art that represents that sound.

Hit the button above, upload your favourite song or audio, and we’ll generate you an image that “Looks like the music sounds” Try it out!

About the project

This project was created in large part in 24 hours, as a submission to Imperial College London’s 2021 IC Hello World hackathon.

We really wanted to explore the more creative and artistic side of computing, which is often overlooked in favour of its more prevalent scientific uses. We had an idea that we found really exciting, and we rolled with it.

SeeMyFeels takes as input an audio file, analyses its characteristics using a pre-trained neural network, and then uses those characteristics to generate images that we feel represent that music.
